# Self-Improvement Prompt (Manual — NotebookLM Web UI)

{{OWNER_NAME}} pastes this into NotebookLM's web interface monthly for blind spot detection. Works with both Operating Brain and Intellectual Capital notebooks.

## Prompt (paste into NotebookLM chat)

```
Act as my strategic advisor. Based on everything in this notebook, give me a brutally honest assessment:

1. BLIND SPOTS: What am I consistently avoiding or not addressing? What patterns show up in my decisions that I might not be aware of?

2. EXECUTION GAPS: Where is the gap between what I say I'll do and what I actually do? What commitments keep slipping?

3. TEAM RISK: Based on the team data, who am I over-investing in despite evidence? Who am I under-investing in despite potential?

4. STRATEGIC DRIFT: Am I staying true to my stated 90-day sprint goals, or am I chasing new things? Where am I spreading too thin?

5. LEVERAGE POINTS: What single change would create the most positive cascading effect across all my ventures?

6. WHAT I'M NOT SEEING: What connections, risks, or opportunities exist in the data that I haven't explicitly addressed?

Be specific. Reference actual content from the notebook. No flattery. No generic frameworks. Just the truth.
```

## When to Use

- Monthly (first Sunday of the month, during `/review`)
- After major strategic pivots
- When feeling stuck or overwhelmed
- Paste directly into NotebookLM web UI for interactive follow-up
